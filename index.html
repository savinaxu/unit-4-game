Shopping for Food

Instructions

You will be given a random allowance at the start of the game.

There are four food you can buy below. By clicking on the food, you will add a specific amount of money to your total costs.

You win the game by spending out all the money you have at the beginning.(allowance === costs)

The price of each food is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each Time when the game starts the game will change the balues of each food.
